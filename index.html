DEVAPP AI Solutions — Privacy Policy for Facebook/Meta Apps

Last updated: November 16, 2025 • Use for Messenger agents & Meta-integrated apps

DEVAPP AI Solutions ("we," "our," "us") operates AI-powered software, web apps, and Facebook Messenger agents. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect information when you interact with our products connected to Facebook/Meta technologies. By using our Facebook app, Messenger bot, or any service integrated with Meta, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect Through Facebook/Meta

When users connect with our Facebook/Meta integrations, we may receive the following information depending on permissions granted:

  • Facebook Account Information — name, profile picture, email (if permitted), Page information (if you manage pages), user ID or page-scoped ID.
  • Messenger Interaction Data — messages sent to the bot, quick replies, conversation history required for bot functionality.
  • Technical & Usage Data — IP address, device details, browser type, interaction logs, and analytics.
  • Optional Data — contact details, business information, uploaded files, or form submissions you provide through the bot or app.

2. How We Use Your Facebook/Meta Data

We use data obtained through Meta platforms to:

  • Operate and improve our Messenger agents
  • Personalize user interactions and deliver automated responses
  • Provide customer support and analyze performance
  • Ensure compliance with Meta's platform policies
  • Send notifications or updates related to our services

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your Facebook data. We may share information only with:

  • Service Providers — hosting, security, development, and maintenance partners bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Meta/Facebook Systems — to enable messaging, authentication, and app features.
  • Legal Authorities — if required by law to protect legal rights or safety.

4. Data Storage & Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures such as encryption, access controls, and secure servers to protect data. However, no transmission method is perfectly secure; use of the service implies acceptance of this risk.

5. Permissions & User Control on Facebook

Users may at any time review or remove permissions granted to our app via Facebook Settings > Business Integrations, block or delete the Messenger bot, or request deletion of personal data (see Data Deletion).

6. Data Retention

We retain Meta data only as long as necessary to provide app/bot functionality, comply with Meta policies, address technical issues, or meet legal obligations. Users can request deletion at any time.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors; if discovered, such data will be deleted promptly.

8. Data Deletion Instructions (Required by Facebook)

To request deletion of your data, follow either option:

  • Option 1: Through Facebook — Remove the app in Facebook Settings > Apps and Websites.
  • Option 2: Contacting Us — Email: devappaisolutions@gmail.com. Include your full name, User ID or Page ID, and the app/bot name ("DEVAPP AI Solutions"). We will permanently delete your data within 7–30 days of verification.

9. Third-Party Links

Our bot or app may include links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third parties. Review their policies before sharing personal data.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy to stay compliant with Meta’s platform requirements and applicable law. We will post the updated policy here with a new date.
